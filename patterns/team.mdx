## Available Team Patterns

Aegis includes team display patterns:

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Team Grid | Team member grid layout |

## Team Grid

Display team members in a responsive grid:

- Photo/avatar
- Name and title
- Bio or description
- Social links

### Structure

```html
<!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
  <!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
  <h2>Meet Our Team</h2>
  <!-- /wp:heading -->
  
  <!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
  <p>The people behind our success.</p>
  <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
  
  <!-- wp:columns -->
    <!-- Team Member 1 -->
    <!-- wp:column -->
      <!-- wp:group {"className":"team-card"} -->
        <!-- wp:image {"className":"team-photo"} /-->
        <!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"textAlign":"center"} -->Jane Doe<!-- /wp:heading -->
        <!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center","className":"team-title"} -->
        <p>CEO & Founder</p>
        <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
        <!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
        <p>Brief bio about Jane and her role.</p>
        <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
        <!-- wp:social-links {"layout":{"type":"flex","justifyContent":"center"}} -->
          <!-- wp:social-link {"url":"#","service":"linkedin"} /-->
          <!-- wp:social-link {"url":"#","service":"twitter"} /-->
        <!-- /wp:social-links -->
      <!-- /wp:group -->
    <!-- /wp:column -->
    
    <!-- Additional team members -->
  <!-- /wp:columns -->
<!-- /wp:group -->
```

## Layout Variations

### Grid Layouts

- **3 columns**: Standard for 3-6 team members
- **4 columns**: For larger teams
- **2 columns**: More detailed bios

### Card Styles

- **Minimal**: Photo, name, title only
- **Standard**: Includes short bio
- **Detailed**: Full bio with social links
- **Hover reveal**: Bio appears on hover

## Customizing Team Patterns

### Photos

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare images">
    Use consistent dimensions (e.g., 400x400px square).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply styling">
    Use border-radius for circular or rounded photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize">
    Compress images for fast loading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Photo Styles

```css
/* Circular photos */
.team-photo img {
  border-radius: 50%;
}

/* Rounded corners */
.team-photo img {
  border-radius: var(--wp--custom--border--radius);
}
```

### Content Elements

Customize what information to display:

- **Name**: Full name or first name only
- **Title**: Job title or role
- **Bio**: 1-2 sentences about the person
- **Social links**: Professional profiles
- **Email**: Direct contact (optional)
- **Skills**: Areas of expertise

### Social Links

Use the Social Links block:

```html
<!-- wp:social-links {"iconColor":"primary-500","iconColorValue":"#6c757d"} -->
<ul class="wp-block-social-links has-icon-color">
  <!-- wp:social-link {"url":"https://linkedin.com/in/username","service":"linkedin"} /-->

  <!-- wp:social-link {"url":"mailto:email@example.com","service":"mail"} /-->

```

## Best Practices

### Photos

- **Consistent style**: Same background, lighting, framing
- **Professional quality**: High-resolution, well-lit
- **Approachable**: Friendly expressions
- **Current**: Recent photos

### Content

- **Concise bios**: 2-3 sentences maximum
- **Personality**: Let individual voices shine
- **Relevant info**: Focus on role and expertise
- **Updated**: Keep information current

<Tip>
Consider adding a fun fact or personal interest to make team members more relatable.
</Tip>

### Accessibility

- Include alt text describing each photo
- Use proper heading hierarchy
- Ensure social links are keyboard accessible
- Provide sufficient color contrast

### Privacy

- Get consent for photos and bios
- Consider what personal info to share
- Allow team members to review their content
- Provide opt-out for social links
