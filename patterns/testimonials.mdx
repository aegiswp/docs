## Available Testimonial Patterns

Aegis includes 4 testimonial patterns:

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Testimonials Grid | Multi-column testimonial cards |
| Testimonials Carousel | Sliding testimonial carousel |
| Testimonials Single | Featured single testimonial |
| Testimonials Quote | Large quote-style testimonial |

## Testimonials Grid

Display multiple testimonials in a grid:

- 2 or 3 column layout
- Avatar, name, and role
- Quote text
- Optional company logo

### Structure

```html
<!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
  <!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
  <h2>What Our Customers Say</h2>
  <!-- /wp:heading -->
  
  <!-- wp:columns -->
    <!-- wp:column -->
      <!-- wp:group {"className":"testimonial-card"} -->
        <!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"testimonial-quote"} -->
        <p>"Amazing product that transformed our workflow."</p>
        <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
        
        <!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"flex"}} -->
          <!-- wp:image {"className":"avatar"} /-->
          <!-- wp:group -->
            <!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"name"} -->Jane Doe<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
            <!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"role"} -->CEO, Company<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
          <!-- /wp:group -->
        <!-- /wp:group -->
      <!-- /wp:group -->
    <!-- /wp:column -->
    
    <!-- Additional testimonials -->
  <!-- /wp:columns -->
<!-- /wp:group -->
```

## Testimonials Carousel

A sliding carousel for multiple testimonials:

- Space-efficient for many testimonials
- Navigation arrows or dots
- Auto-play option

<Note>
The carousel uses the Aegis Slider block. See [Custom Blocks](/blocks/custom-blocks) for details.
</Note>

## Customizing Testimonials

### Avatar Images

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select avatar">
    Click on the avatar image placeholder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replace image">
    Upload or select a customer photo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust styling">
    Apply circular crop using border-radius.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Quote Styling

Enhance quote appearance:

- Use italic text for quotes
- Add quotation marks via CSS or text
- Apply subtle background colors
- Add left border for visual interest

### Star Ratings

Add star ratings to testimonials:

```html
<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"star-rating"} -->
<p>★★★★★</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
```

Or use an icon-based approach with the Icon block.

## Best Practices

### Content

- **Authentic quotes**: Use real customer feedback
- **Specific results**: Include metrics when possible
- **Relevant roles**: Show job titles and companies
- **Permission**: Always get approval to use testimonials

### Design

- **Consistent styling**: Same card design for all testimonials
- **Readable text**: Appropriate font size for quotes
- **Visual hierarchy**: Name and role less prominent than quote
- **White space**: Don't overcrowd testimonial cards

### Accessibility

- Include alt text for avatar images
- Ensure quote text has sufficient contrast
- Make carousel controls keyboard accessible

<Tip>
Feature your strongest testimonials prominently. Quality over quantity—3 great testimonials are better than 10 mediocre ones.
</Tip>

## Social Proof Variations

Combine testimonials with:

- **Logos**: Display client company logos
- **Stats**: "500+ happy customers"
- **Ratings**: Average rating from review platforms
- **Case studies**: Link to detailed success stories
