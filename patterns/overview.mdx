## What are Patterns?

Patterns are pre-designed page components that provide a quick method for building sections or entire page layouts. Instead of building from scratch, you can leverage Aegis's patterns for efficient website design.

## Using Patterns

### In the Block Editor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Inserter">
    Click the **+** button or press `/` in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Patterns">
    Click the **Patterns** tab in the inserter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Browse categories or search for specific patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert">
    Click a pattern to insert it at your cursor position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Steps>

## Pattern Categories

Aegis patterns are registered with WordPress default categories:

- `featured` - Highlighted patterns
- `header` - Header patterns
- `footer` - Footer patterns
- `call-to-action` - CTA sections
- `testimonials` - Testimonial sections
- `team` - Team displays
- `gallery` - Gallery layouts
- `posts` - Blog and post patterns
- `text` - Text-focused layouts
- `about` - About sections
- `contact` - Contact sections

## Creating Full Pages with Patterns

Build complete pages by stacking patterns:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new page">
    Add a new page in WordPress.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select template">
    Choose the **No Page Title** template for full control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add hero pattern">
    Insert a hero pattern at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content sections">
    Stack feature, testimonial, CTA patterns as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit text, images, and colors to match your brand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use the **No Page Title** template when building pages with patterns to avoid duplicate titles, but always include an H1 heading in your content for SEO.
</Tip>

## Pattern Best Practices

- **Use presets** for colors, spacing, and typography
- **Remove IDs** from images for reusability
- **Include alt text** for accessibility
- **Use translation functions** for internationalization
- **Test responsiveness** on multiple screen sizes
