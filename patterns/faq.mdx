## Available FAQ Patterns

Aegis includes 3 FAQ patterns:

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| FAQ Accordion | Expandable accordion-style FAQ |
| FAQ Two Column | Side-by-side FAQ layout |
| FAQ Simple | Basic list-style FAQ |

## FAQ Accordion

An interactive accordion where users click to expand answers:

- Space-efficient
- Users find specific questions easily
- Clean, organized appearance

### Structure

```html
<!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
  <!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
  <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
  <!-- /wp:heading -->
  
  <!-- wp:details -->
  <details>
    <summary>What is your refund policy?</summary>
    <!-- wp:paragraph -->
    <p>We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.</p>
    <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
  </details>
  <!-- /wp:details -->
  
  <!-- Additional FAQ items -->
<!-- /wp:group -->
```

<Note>
Aegis uses the native WordPress Details block for accordions, ensuring accessibility and no JavaScript dependency.
</Note>

## FAQ Two Column

A two-column layout for longer FAQ sections:

- Questions and answers side by side
- Good for comprehensive FAQ pages
- Visual balance

### Structure

```html
<!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
  <!-- wp:columns -->
    <!-- wp:column -->
      <!-- FAQ items 1-5 -->
    <!-- /wp:column -->
    
    <!-- wp:column -->
      <!-- FAQ items 6-10 -->
    <!-- /wp:column -->
  <!-- /wp:columns -->
<!-- /wp:group -->
```

## FAQ Simple

A straightforward list of questions and answers:

- All content visible
- No interaction required
- Good for short FAQ sections

### Structure

```html
<!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
  <!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
  <h3>What is your refund policy?</h3>
  <!-- /wp:heading -->
  
  <!-- wp:paragraph -->
  <p>We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee...</p>
  <!-- /wp:paragraph -->
  
  <!-- wp:separator /-->
  
  <!-- Additional Q&A pairs -->
<!-- /wp:group -->
```

## Customizing FAQs

### Accordion Styling

Customize the Details block appearance:

- Border styles for visual separation
- Background colors for expanded state
- Icon customization for open/close indicator

Aegis provides custom properties for accordion styling:

```css
--wp--custom--accordion--height: 1.5px;
--wp--custom--accordion--width: 12px;
--wp--custom--accordion--animation-duration: 0s;
```

### Categories

Organize FAQs by category:

```html
<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3>Billing & Payments</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- Billing FAQs -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3>Technical Support</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- Support FAQs -->
```

### Search

For large FAQ sections, consider adding a search block:

```html
<!-- wp:search {"label":"Search FAQs","placeholder":"Type your question..."} /-->
```

## Best Practices

### Content

- **Clear questions**: Write questions as users would ask them
- **Concise answers**: Get to the point quickly
- **Complete information**: Answer the full question
- **Links**: Reference related pages or documentation

### Organization

- **Most common first**: Put frequently asked questions at the top
- **Logical grouping**: Organize by topic or category
- **Limit length**: 10-15 questions per section maximum

### SEO Benefits

FAQs can improve SEO:

- Target long-tail keywords
- Appear in featured snippets
- Add FAQ schema markup

<Tip>
Use actual customer questions from support tickets, emails, or chat logs for authentic FAQ content.
</Tip>

### Accessibility

- Ensure accordion controls are keyboard accessible
- Use proper heading hierarchy
- Provide sufficient color contrast
- Test with screen readers
