## Welcome to Aegis

Aegis is a cutting-edge Full Site Editing (FSE) theme that combines performance with aesthetics. Built with zero CSS, JavaScript, or font assets loaded by default, Aegis delivers a pure architectural foundation for high-performance WordPress sites.
